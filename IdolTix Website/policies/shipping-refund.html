Shipping & Refund Policy

Fulfillment and refund handling.

Last updated: 22 April 2026

This policy applies to order requests for trading card accessories and hobby goods sold by IDOLTIX, UEN 53522050B.

Order processing

After you submit an order request, IdolTix will confirm product availability, quantity, delivery or collection option, and any applicable delivery fees before the order is finalized. Processing typically takes 1 to 3 business days after order confirmation.

Singapore delivery and collection

IdolTix currently supports Singapore-based fulfillment. Available options may include local delivery, meetup, or self-collection by appointment, depending on order size and customer location. Final arrangements are confirmed directly with the customer before fulfillment.

Delivery fees

Product prices are shown on the website in SGD. Delivery fees, if applicable, are confirmed before the order is accepted and fulfilled.

Refunds and replacements

Please inspect items promptly after receipt. If you receive an incorrect item or an item damaged before delivery, contact IdolTix within 3 calendar days with your order details and clear photos where relevant.

If the issue is verified, IdolTix may offer a replacement, store credit, or refund depending on stock availability and the nature of the issue.

Change of mind

Refunds are not generally provided for change of mind after an order has been confirmed, packed, or fulfilled. Exceptions may be considered at IdolTix's discretion before fulfillment begins.
